Eligiblity

  • Students should pass the BE Entrance Examination conducted by IOE, TU.
  • Students must have completed I.Sc. or +2 (Science) or Diploma in Engineering (3 Years) or equivalent with minimum 45% or C grade in all subjects.

OAPAS Guideline
For admission to BE, Khwopa College of Engineering, an applicant should go through Online Admission Priority Application Submission (OAPAS). There are four steps you have to complete for submitting your application:

  1. Enter Basic Information
  2. Upload IOE Score Card & Citizenship Certificate
  3. Upload Bank Voucher & Select Priority
  4. Complete the form with the remaining Information

Bank Account Name: Khwopa College of Engineering, Nepal Bank Ltd. 
Account Number: 01900100035573000001 

The applicant needs to pay Rs. 200 for single priority. If s/he wants to choose additional priorities then s/he should pay Rs. 100 for each priorities.  Payment can be done directly at the College Account Section (Block E, Ground Floor) too.

Already submitted? Track your progress or check your name in verified applicants' list.

Pay Online Form Fee via eSewa! The applicant needs to pay Rs. 200 for single priority. If s/he wants to choose all three priorities then s/he should pay Rs. 400.
