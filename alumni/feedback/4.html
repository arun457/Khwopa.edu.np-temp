Er. Imjal Sukupayo

I am very happy to be a part of Khwopa College of Engineering (KhCE). As an undergraduate student, I met incredible, hardworking, friendly and dedicated faculties, working as a team with a pride. They provided me enormous opportunities in the research platforms and it has always been fruitful working with them. Likewise, I got many opportunities to participate in different engineering competition, exhibitions and conferences and to chance to cope with the Professors from abroad.  Beside this, pristine environment, well quipped laboratories, frequent guest lectures, rigorous field works and ample resourced library always fascinated me during my bachelor’s program. Apart from this, quality education, provision of semester topper scholarship motivated me to be in track of good academic excellence.
Various engineering software taught in college made me easy and comfortable to perform my professional works as Design Engineer in Hydropower Sector for four years in Nepal. Education in KhCE gave me a confidence to complete my engineering works more precisely and accurately, and made me able to take leadership position in my job. Also, KhCE has taught me about the being responsible for the society and people. It has taught me that life is not getting and having, it’s about giving and taking.  I want to thank the college admiration for running the college regularly in the bandhas and strikes too. I highly recommend this college as  one of the prominent engineering college to anyone who dream of professional engineers with academic transcendence in affordable fees. KhCE is the right destination in every regard to chase one’s dream career.
I am about to peruse my Masters in Water Resource Engineering in Lund University at Sweden in Full Scholarship from SI Institute. Lund University is World’s Top 100 University with more than 350 years of Academics. For achieving this, I am heartily thankful to my college and hardworking Principal, Faculties who continuously inspired and motivated in the process of acquiring higher education. I am proud of being member of Almuni of Khwopa college of Engineering.

Er. Imjal Sukupayo, Batch - 2068
Graduate Student
Water Resource Engineering
Lund University, Skane, Sweden
